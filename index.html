Get to know me

Hi there! I'm Derrik Wolthuis. A front-end developer from the Netherlands. I'm a full-time code monkey at Trendwerk, where I help develop WordPress websites.

For the past ten years I've been building websites experiences. I specialize in responsive front-end development, HTML5, CSS (Sass), web typography and WordPress. I'm always looking for ways to improve myself and am currently working on my JavaScript skills.

In 2012 I graduated at the Saxion University of Applied Art & Technology. For my graduation I wrote a thesis on webdevelopment frameworks while developing Trendwerk's own framework TrendPress which is still actively developed today.

When I'm not pixel wrangling you'll find me spending time with my lovely family, watching TV shows, listening to music or the latest podcast, gardening and maybe even some napping.

If you want to know what I am up to right now you should follow me on Twitter, connect with me on LinkedIn.
